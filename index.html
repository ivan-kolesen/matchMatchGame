Match-match game

Welcome to match-match game!

Game rules:

  • Select the card and flip it over
  • Select another card and also flip it
  • If the images match, the cards will disappear
  • Otherwise the cards will flip over back
  • Your task is to find all pairs of cards and clear the field
  • Good luck!!!
